## Sleep is your super power

### Question 4 : Paraphrase the Sleep is your Superpower video in your own words in brief. Only the points, no explanation
- not enough sleep is bad for body and brain
- less sleep weakens immune system.
- deep sleep moves memories to long-term storage
- sleep is very important, not a luxury
- sleep before learning helps brain learn better

### Question 5: What are some ideas that you can implement to sleep better?
- keep bedroom cool
- avoiding heavy meals at night
- avoid daytime naps if they disturb night sleep
- reduce screen time before sleep
- sleep and wake up at same time every day
