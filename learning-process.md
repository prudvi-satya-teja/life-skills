#### 3. What are active and diffused modes of thinking?

Active (Focused) Mode:
1. Deep concentration on one problem.  
2. Step-by-step logical thinking.  
3. Used while solving coding or math problems.  

Diffused Mode:
1. Relaxed thinking state.  
2. Brain works in the background.  
3. Helps in making new connections.
