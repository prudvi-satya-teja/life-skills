## Grit and Growth Mindset

### 1. Grit

* Grit means staying passionate and committed to your long-term goals.
* Working hard consistently is more important than relying only on talent.
* Overcoming obstacles is part of staying dedicated.
* Keeping focus on your goal helps achieve success over time.

### 2. Introduction to Growth Mindset

* Growth mindset is believing you can improve your skills with effort.
* Challenges are opportunities to learn and grow.
* Mistakes are chances to gain experience.
* Persistence and learning lead to better results.

### 3. Understanding Internal Locus of Control

* Internal locus of control means you believe your actions affect outcomes.
* Taking responsibility helps you stay motivated.
* You focus on what you can control rather than blaming others.
* Believing in your influence encourages consistent effort.

### 4. How to Build a Growth Mindset

* Face challenges instead of avoiding them.
* Learn from failures and use them to improve.
* Keep trying even when it gets difficult.
* Listen to feedback and make changes to grow.

### 5. Ideas to Take Action and Build Growth Mindset

* Set small goals and track your progress regularly.
* Try difficult tasks to push your limits.
* Get advice or feedback and apply it to improve.
* See failures as learning experiences rather than setbacks.
